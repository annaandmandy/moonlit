    The Qingqiu Murder

    On the night of the lunar eclipse, the village priest Aqi was found lifeless on the Qingqiu altar— blood drained, skull cracked, a serpent mark burned into the stone. The Eastern Wilderness now shudders as rumors spread of fox spirits and multi-headed shadows.

    You arrive moments after the ritual collapsed. Baize, your immortal companion, has cataloged every beast in the Classic of Mountains and Seas and will guide you through each crime scene.
